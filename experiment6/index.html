Experiment 6 - Grammars & Text Art

Description

Switches between different gifs on a timer, flashing a word in the center of the screen and saying it.

Technical

A random GIF is displayed every 3 seconds by storing URLs of GIFs and loading them into an array where they are pulled onto the screen. The name of the tag is flashed on top of the GIF, and spoken using a text-to-speech function.

Reflection

Fun to figure out how to make it all work by dissecting code and seeing what made GIFs load, also fun to make a 'ludovico technique' simulator.
