Experiment 4 - Images, Videos & Sound Art

Description

Simulates the Necker Cube optical illusion as it spins around.

Technical

The Necker Cube consists of 2D vectors which constantly rotate at a slow speed. It consists of a front and back face, which spin around and occasionally change color based on a timer to change appearances behind looking like they're visibly in front & behind.

Reflection

Had a lot of fun with this assignment and trying to make something entirely new. I don't think I fully nailed the optical illusion but I think I did what I could within the reasonable scope of p5.js.
